How to make Egg & Tomato Pandesal

Description

Traditional pandesal with sauteed egg and tomatoes as palaman (what goes in a bread, such as a spread), perfectly eaten for breakfast with coffee.

Ingredients

  • pandesal
  • eggs, 4 pieces
  • tomatoes, 3 pieces
  • garlic, 2 cloves
  • onion, 1 bulb
  • butter, 2 tablespoons
  • MSG, to taste
  • salt, to taste
  • black pepper, to taste
  • rosemary or thyme, to taste

Steps

  1. Slice the onion into thin slices. Slice the tomatoes into small medium-sized cubes. Mince the garlic finely.
  2. Saute the onion, tomato, and garlic in a pan with butter. Add a little water to help soften the tomatoes. Season with herbs and black pepper to taste.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs, adding in salt to your preference.
  4. Crush the tomatoes with your spatula and add in the beaten eggs.
  5. Scramble the egg and tomato mixture until the egg develops some structure. Doneness and how stiff the eggs would be depend on your preference.
  6. Once cooked, transfer to a plate. Open the pandesal on its side and fill it with eggs. Enjoy!
