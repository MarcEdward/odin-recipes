How to cook Kangkong Sinangag

Description

Flavorful fried rice cooked with kangkong (water spinach), perfectly eaten as is or with other breakfast meats.

Ingredients

  • day-old rice, 3 cups
  • kangkong (water spinach), 5 stems
  • eggs, 3 pieces
  • garlic, 4 cloves
  • onion (small), 1 head
  • cooking oil, 3 tablespoons
  • soy sauce, 3 tablespoons
  • oyster sauce, 2 tablespoons
  • bagoong (shrimp paste), 1 tablespoon
  • MSG, to taste
  • sugar, 2 teaspoons
  • black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Chop the kangkong, including its stem. Mince the onion and garlic.
  2. Saute the onion, garlic, and kangkong on a pan with cooking oil. Continue until aroma develops.
  3. Beat the eggs and season with salt. Set the kangkong to the side of the pan and fry the beaten eggs. Once cooked, break the eggs into small chunks.
  4. Add in the rice, bagoong, soy sauce, and oyster sauce. Mix thoroughly.
  5. Season with MSG, sugar, and black pepper, and continue to mix the fried rice.
  6. Let the rice toast, but don't let it burn too much. How much you toast it depends on your preference. This step can be optional.
  7. Plate the fried rice in a bowl. Enjoy!
